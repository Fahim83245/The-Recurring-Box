What’s included in my monthly subscription?

Each plan includes unlimited content edits, analytics, custom email domains, SEO optimization, Google My Business profile support, and access to your dedicated team. Higher-tier plans offer additional features like increased CMS collections, higher form submissions, and enhanced bandwidth. You can view the full list of features on the pricing page.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

Yes, each plan requires a minimum of a six-month lock-in period. After this initial period, you can switch to month-to-month billing or explore annual pricing options.

How quickly are requests completed?

Our team strives for fast turnarounds, with the goal of completing most tasks within 1-2 business days, depending on the task’s complexity. For more extensive requests, we’ll provide an estimated completion time in advance.

Are design edits unlimited too?

While content edits, such as photos or text, are unlimited, design edits are limited to ensure no abuse of service. Contact us for the specifics.

What happens if I need more bandwidth or form submissions?

If you exceed your plan’s bandwidth or form submission limits, you can purchase additional capacity at a competitive rate. Plans also include surge protection, and our team will notify you if your needs frequently exceed your current plan, in which case a upgrade of your plan may be recommended.

What are the email specifications?

For our static plan, you get 10 email addresses on your domain with 5GB storage. Your domain (including all emails) are able to take 200 emails in and 50 emails out per day. However, for our dynamic plan, you receive unlimited email addresses with 30GB storage. This domain will now handle 1,000 emails in and 100 emails out per day.

Can you help redesign my site?

Unfortunately, we see that many websites rely on incompatible infrastructure. We always start fresh to be able to maintain our level of quality.

Do I own my website and its content?

It’s a simplified transition. We own the infrastructure and the website but you own the design. This allows us to take care of the technicalities and this makes sure you’re not tied down-you can use your design wherever you like. We want to keep you here because you want to be here, not because you have to be. If you ever want to move your design somewhere else, that’s totally possible, just contact us in advance!

What about refunds?

Due to the nature of our subscription model, refunds are a bit like unicorns—magical but sadly non-existent! But don’t worry, we’re committed to refining things until you’re happy.

What are credits?

Get half-month credit on a web development plan with each booked in referral. Cannot be used consecutively.

What if I need support outside of business hours?

You have a direct line of contact via WhatsApp for urgent requests, and we aim to address these texts as quickly as possible. For general requests, we respond within 24 hours during business days.
